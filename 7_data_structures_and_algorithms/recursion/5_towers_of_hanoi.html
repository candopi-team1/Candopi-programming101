This puzzle uses 3 poles and a few disks of different sizes.

You move all the disks from the first pole to the third pole according to the following rules :
1.  Only one disk can be moved at a time.
2.  You can only move the top disc in a stack.
3.  No disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk.
